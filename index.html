About Us

At Sunrise Café, we believe in serving fresh, homemade food that makes every day a little brighter. Nestled in the heart of downtown, we've been providing a cozy space for locals to gather, enjoy good food, and relax since 2010.


Our mission is to create a warm, welcoming environment where everyone feels like family. Whether you're grabbing a quick breakfast before work or enjoying a leisurely lunch with friends, we've got something for everyone.


Join us for a delicious meal and experience the unique charm of Sunrise Café. We promise you'll leave with a smile on your face!
